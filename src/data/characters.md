# The Legend of Zelda - Characters

## Link
Link is the main protagonist of The Legend of Zelda series.
He is a courageous young hero who is often chosen by destiny to save Hyrule.
Link wields the Master Sword, also known as the Blade of Evil's Bane.
He carries the Triforce of Courage within him.
Link is a member of the Hylian race and is often reincarnated across different eras.
His iconic green tunic and pointed hat are his most recognizable features.
In Breath of the Wild, Link is a knight who awakens with no memories after 100 years of slumber.
In Ocarina of Time, Link begins as a young boy living in Kokiri Forest.

## Zelda
Princess Zelda is the princess of Hyrule and a central figure in the series.
She carries the Triforce of Wisdom, granting her great knowledge and foresight.
Zelda is often a reincarnation of the goddess Hylia.
In Ocarina of Time, Zelda disguises herself as the Sheikah warrior Sheik.
In Breath of the Wild, Zelda is a scholar who studies ancient technology called Sheikah Slate.
She has the power of sealing Calamity Ganon through her sacred power.
Zelda often communicates with Link through visions or telepathy.

## Ganon / Ganondorf
Ganondorf is the primary antagonist of the Legend of Zelda series.
He is the King of the Gerudo, a desert-dwelling tribe of warrior women.
Ganondorf carries the Triforce of Power, making him nearly invincible.
He seeks to obtain all three pieces of the Triforce to conquer Hyrule.
When overwhelmed by dark magic, Ganondorf transforms into the beast Ganon.
In Ocarina of Time, Ganondorf is the first Gerudo male born in 100 years.
In Breath of the Wild, he is known as Calamity Ganon, a manifestation of pure malice.

## Impa
Impa is a loyal protector of Princess Zelda and a member of the Sheikah tribe.
She serves as a guardian, advisor, and sage across multiple games.
In Ocarina of Time, she is the Sage of Shadow.
In Skyward Sword, Impa is a mysterious figure who guides Link on his journey.
She is one of the few characters to appear in many different Zelda timelines.

## Navi
Navi is a fairy companion assigned to Link in Ocarina of Time.
She provides guidance and advice throughout Link's adventure.
Navi is known for her iconic phrase "Hey! Listen!".
She helps Link target enemies using the Z-targeting system.
At the end of Ocarina of Time, Navi mysteriously disappears.

## Midna
Midna is the Twilight Princess and a companion to Link in Twilight Princess.
She is the true ruler of the Twilight Realm, cursed into imp form by Zant.
Midna is witty, sarcastic, and initially self-serving but grows to care for Link.
She wields the Fused Shadows, powerful ancient artifacts of the Twili people.
At the end of Twilight Princess, Midna destroys the Mirror of Twilight severing the connection between worlds.

## Fi
Fi is a spirit that resides within the Goddess Sword (which becomes the Master Sword) in Skyward Sword.
She was created by the goddess Hylia to assist the chosen hero.
Fi communicates in a logical, analytical manner and provides statistical analysis.
She eventually enters an eternal sleep inside the Master Sword at the end of Skyward Sword.

## Urbosa
Urbosa is the Gerudo Champion and pilot of the Divine Beast Vah Naboris in Breath of the Wild.
She is a close friend of Queen Sonia and a mentor figure to Princess Zelda.
Urbosa wields the power of lightning and commands her weapon Scimitar of the Seven.
She was killed by Thunderblight Ganon 100 years before the events of Breath of the Wild.

## Revali
Revali is the Rito Champion and pilot of the Divine Beast Vah Medoh in Breath of the Wild.
He is arrogant and competitive but deeply skilled with a bow.
Revali created the ability Revali's Gale, allowing Link to launch into the air.
He was killed by Windblight Ganon 100 years before the events of Breath of the Wild.

## Daruk
Daruk is the Goron Champion and pilot of the Divine Beast Vah Rudania in Breath of the Wild.
He is warm, cheerful, and protective of those he cares about.
Daruk wields the Boulder Breaker and possesses Daruk's Protection, a powerful shield.
He was killed by Fireblight Ganon 100 years before the events of Breath of the Wild.

## Mipha
Mipha is the Zora Champion and pilot of the Divine Beast Vah Ruta in Breath of the Wild.
She is gentle, kind, and deeply in love with Link.
Mipha has healing powers and crafted the Zora Armor specifically for Link.
She was killed by Waterblight Ganon 100 years before the events of Breath of the Wild.

## Rauru
Rauru is the first King of Hyrule and a Sage of Light.
In Ocarina of Time, he maintains the Sacred Realm and guides Link as an adult.
In Tears of the Kingdom, Rauru is revealed as the ancient Hylian king who founded Hyrule.
He possesses the Triforce on his right hand and wields the power of light.
